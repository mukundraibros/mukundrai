M/s Mukundrai Bros. (Pharmacy since 77 years)

In 1940 Mukundrai Brothers Medical Store started from humble beginnings to serve the needy in Nagpur. The founder of the firm, were three brothers of Vora family Vasantrai, Mukundrai and Bhogilal. Three of them served as chemist since pre-independence era. All three brothers left for heavenly abode but their values and principles are still followed by the next generations.

Today, Mukundrai Brothers is oldest and trusted pharmacies in Nagpur. 

The store deals with variety of products ranging from 

  • Allopathic medicines

  • Ayurvedic formulations

  • Cosmetics

  • Neutraceuticals

  • Surgical equipments
