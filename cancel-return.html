Goods Returns Policy


Items are eligible for return if:
  • Item received is different from what was ordered/prescribed
  • In case of diagnostic equipments if they are damaged, defective or malfunctioning or some parts/accessories are missing
  • Only unopened items can be returned
  • Cut/loose strips cannot be returned
  • It is mandatory to present original invoice while returning items
  • Non-returnable items include syringes, needles and injections
  • The batch number of item to be returned must match the batch number mentioned in the invoice
  • Item are eligible for return upto 30 days from date of purchase to receive store credit
  • Generic medicines cannot be returned
  • Medicines are physically damaged (For example capsule is crushed or seal is open\tampered-with in case of creams etc.)
  • Medicines received have a shelf life of less than 3 months from the date of reciept
  • Items especially ordered for customer and made available (manual reorder items) will not be eligible for return

How to return an item or cancel an order?

  • If you wish to cancel an order you already placed call on 0712-2722478 to cancel your order.
  • To return an item you need to bring the item to our store between 2pm to 4pm. (See address here.)
  • When you return an item we will issue store credit which you can redeem on your next purchase.
